Crime Prevention Project [Web-Portal]

Crowd-Sourced Crime Reporting Platform in Toronto Area

CrimeSolutions is a platform for crime reporting to supplement crime activity databases. This platform provides online access, visualization, spatial analysis and data sharing services of Crowd-Sourced Crime data to give perspectives on crime patterns in Toronto areas. The project is aimed at contributing to social science research on crimes and crime prevention. We trust this platform will be helpful to our targeted audience including, but not limited to, police officers, local communities, governments, and researchers. Your report is very valuable. See something, say something!
